What materials are used in your compostable bags?

Our compostable bags are made using plant-based materials such as cornstarch and PBAT, designed to break down naturally without leaving harmful microplastics.

Are your products eco-friendly and safe for daily use?

Yes, our bags and waste solutions are manufactured following environmentally responsible practices and are safe for household, commercial, and food-handling applications.

Are compostable garbage bags strong enough for wet waste?

Yes, our garbage bags are engineered for durability, offering leak-resistant and tear-resistant performance suitable for kitchen, office, and commercial waste handling.

Are your dustbins compatible with compostable liners?

Absolutely. Our dustbins are designed to work seamlessly with compostable garbage bags, making waste segregation and disposal more hygienic and sustainable.

Do you offer custom sizes or branding for products?

Yes, we provide customized sizes, thickness options, and logo printing solutions for businesses and bulk buyers to match branding and operational needs.

Do you supply products in bulk for businesses or institutions?

Yes, we support bulk manufacturing and nationwide supply for retail chains, offices, hospitals, hotels, and industrial clients with consistent quality and reliable delivery.
