Starting Home Composting

Composting at home allows you to turn kitchen scraps and organic waste into nutrient-rich soil, reducing landfill contributions.

Home Composting Tips

  • Use compostable bins for kitchen waste.
  • Maintain a balance of greens (food scraps) and browns (dry leaves, paper).
  • Keep compost moist and aerated for faster decomposition.
  • Use the finished compost in gardens and plants.

By composting at home, you can actively reduce your environmental footprint while producing natural fertilizer for your garden.
