Who We Are

Manufactured with Responsibility, Designed for the Planet.

Ecoenvi is a specialized manufacturing unit focused on producing high-quality biodegradable and compostable bags made from plant-based corn starch materials. We manufacture a wide range of sustainable packaging and waste-management solutions, including compostable carry bags, grocery and shopping bags, industrial compostable garbage bags and food-grade packaging solutions offering businesses a complete eco-friendly packaging solution under one roof.

Driven by sustainability rather than profit alone, our mission is to replace conventional plastic with responsible alternatives and support India’s transition toward a greener, cleaner future.

Plant-based raw materials using cornstarch & PBAT resin.

Custom branding available with organic water-based inks.

Pan-India supply capability with bulk manufacturing capacity.
